If your battery needs to be replaced:

  1. Read the Safety guidelines.

  2. Disconnect the power cable from the plug located on NAO’s back.

  3. Remove the four screws of the battery cover. You will need a Phillips 0 screwdriver.

  4. Remove the used battery and replace it with a new one.

  5. Once it fits snuggly in its slot, place the cover back into position and tighten all four screws.

  6. Refer to the Charging the battery section to charge your new battery.
