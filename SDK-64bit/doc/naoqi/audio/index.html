This section describes the audio software components of your robot.

NAOqi currently contains the following audio modules:

  • ALAudioDevice: manages audio inputs and outputs.
  • ALAudioPlayer: allows playing audio files on NAO.
  • ALAudioRecorder: allows recording audio files on NAO.
  • ALTextToSpeech: allows NAO to speak.
  • ALSpeechRecognition: allows NAO to understand what we say.
  • ALSoundDetection: allows detecting sound events.
  • ALAudioSourceLocalization: allows localizing sounds detected by the ALSoundDetection module.
