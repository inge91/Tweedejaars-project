Replacing the battery
============================

.. seealso::

   - :ref:`charging-battery`
   - :ref:`Battery Hardware <hardware-battery>`
   - :ref:`albattery`


If your battery needs to be replaced:

#. Read the :ref:`Safety<nao-safety>` guidelines.

#. Disconnect the power cable from the plug located on NAO's back.

#. Remove the four screws of the battery cover. You will need a Phillips 0 screwdriver.

   .. image:: /medias/nao/gs_vis_dos.png
      :height: 192 px
      :width: 142 px

#. Remove the used battery and replace it with a new one.

   .. image:: /medias/nao/hardware/hardware_battery.jpg
      :height: 250 px
      :width: 250 px

#. Once it fits snuggly in its slot, place the cover back into position and tighten all four screws.

#. Refer to the :ref:`charging-battery` section to charge your new battery.
