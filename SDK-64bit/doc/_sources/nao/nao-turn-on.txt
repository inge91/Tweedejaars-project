Turning your NAO on and off
============================

* To turn NAO on, press his chest button: he will boot up.

* To turn NAO off, press and hold his chest button for 5 seconds. NAO may fall so make sure he's in a safe position.

* To force NAO to perform an emergency shut down (not recommended in normal use) press and hold his chest button for 8 seconds.

.. image:: /medias/nao/turn_on_nao.png

* Two quick consecutive presses removes power from NAO's motors.

  If you do this, hold NAO in your hands as it may fall.
